About me

My name is Youngchan Kim, but my friends know me as Kim! I am a portrait photographer based out of Snowflake Korea. I love to enjoy life with my guitar and a weekly soccer game.

My passions include family, photography, playing guitar, playing soccer, making new friends, and watching movies.

I started photographing people in 2013 & haven't stopped. I love to capture the emotion and tell a story with images. Life is beautiful and I find joy in capturing yours through my camera.

I'd love to meet you and take your photos! If you think we're a good fit, feel free to reach out to me & I'll be in touch.

Thanks for visiting Kim's photo studio!
