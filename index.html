Welcome to Kim's photo studio!

I want you in front of my camera! I want to help you document your candid and true self.

I specialize in photography that mixes a few posed moments (for those who love the modeling) with a bunch of unposed moments (for those who also like to roam free).

I like to make the process comfortable, fun, and as full of laughs as possible. I make an effort to get to know you so by the time that I take out my camera, we’re already old friends.

Take a look around here and don’t be shy to contact me with any questions or to start setting an appointment for your photography session with me. I’m so excited to meet you!
